hello there !

Ikramul Hasan Shakil is an individual whose journey from the rice fields to the summits of the Himalayas is a testament to his determination and passion for mountaineering. Born into a middle-class family, Shakil initially found himself tending to crops in the rice fields, a humble occupation that sustained his family

However, his life took a turn when he discovered his passion for mountaineering. Despite facing numerous challenges, Shakil persevered and became the first Bangladeshi to summit Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world. Shakil's journey is an inspiration to all those who dare to dream and strive for greatness.
